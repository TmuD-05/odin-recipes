Fresh Garden Salad

This fresh garden salad is a quick and healthy recipe full of vibrant vegetables, tossed in a light vinaigrette dressing. Perfect as a side or a light main course!

How to Make a Fresh Garden Salad

Here's what you need and the steps to prepare a refreshing salad at home:

Ingredients
  • For the Salad:
  • 2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/2 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup red onions (thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup croutons (optional)
  • For the Dressing:
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to Follow
  1. Wash and dry all the vegetables thoroughly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine romaine, spinach, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and carrots.
  3. In a small bowl or jar, mix olive oil, vinegar, mustard, honey, salt, and pepper. Shake or whisk until combined.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving and toss gently.
  5. Top with croutons and serve immediately.
