Creamy Banana Strawberry Split Smoothie

This creamy banana strawberry split smoothie is a refreshing, healthy treat that tastes like a dessert — perfect for breakfast, post-workout, or a midday snack!

How to Make a Creamy Banana Strawberry Split Smoothie

Here’s a simple and delicious way to whip up a smoothie in minutes:

Ingredients
  • 1 ripe banana (frozen for best texture)
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt (or Greek yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ice cubes (optional, for extra chill)
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings (optional topping)

Steps to Follow
  1. Peel the banana and slice it into chunks.
  2. Add banana, strawberries, yogurt, milk, honey, and vanilla to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy. Add ice cubes and blend again if a thicker texture is desired.
  4. Pour into a tall glass and top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings if using.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
